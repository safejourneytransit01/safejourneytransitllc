About Us

Safe Journey Transportation LLC is dedicated to connecting drivers with flexible, profitable opportunities in the rideshare industry. We provide vehicles, manage operational logistics, and offer unparalleled support to ensure your success on the road.

Driver Opportunities

We offer flexible shifts, competitive revenue splits, and added incentives for our drivers. Work the hours you want and earn more, all while driving a fully equipped and insured vehicle.

  • 55/45 revenue split in your favor.
  • Weekly gas incentives of $60.
  • Fully insured and equipped vehicle with GPS tracking, dash camera, and Wi-Fi.
  • Keep 100% of your cash tips.
  • Flexible 8-hour or 4-hour shifts for full-time or part-time drivers.

Why Rideshare?

Rideshare services provide a flexible and rewarding way to earn income by connecting passengers with drivers in real time. With millions of users worldwide, ridesharing offers a steady flow of customers and the freedom to work on your own schedule.

  • Set your own hours and earn as much as you want.
  • Meet new people and explore your community.
  • Enjoy the security of working with well-known platforms like Uber and Lyft.
